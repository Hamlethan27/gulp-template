Autonomous MeaD robot

The MeaD Robot has been designed with feedback from specialists involved with cleaning and disinfection procedures. Our autonomous robot has several cutting-edge features.

Smoothness

New hydraulic joints were first installed on a working prototype. The joints made of brand new carbon based material can bend more easily which allows Mead to move smoother.

Humanoid

As bizarre as it sounds to have a live-in robot at your disposal to perform repetitive or precision demanding medicine-connected tasks

Less stress

The introduction of Mead has shown to reduce the levels of stress among medical staff, the decrease varied from 34 to 68 percent depending on the number of robots and degree of depth of human-machine interaction.

Artificial Intelligence

Healthcare Robots - the manufacturer of the autonomous assisting robot equipped with modern AI technologies - was selected by the European Commission, as a supplier for its autonomous nurse robots to European Union hospitals
